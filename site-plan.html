FAQ Does it hurt? Waxing hair removal has been around for a long time. While it can be an uncomfortable experience, our clients are always pleased with their results. How long is a typical service? I book out services for 30 minutes but you can expect your Brazilian wax to take only 15 minutes Can I get waxed while I’m pregnant? Yes up until you give birth. Your Brazilian area will be slightly more sensitive due to pregnancy. How much time between waxes? We encourage clients to return every 4 weeks to get waxed. This helps the hair to get on the same growth cycle to eventually have an easier and smoother wax. For this reason, we encourage clients to not shave between appointments. Waxing vs. Laser Hair removal Waxing is a great alternative to laser hair removal. It’s cheaper and less painful and has better long-term results.
